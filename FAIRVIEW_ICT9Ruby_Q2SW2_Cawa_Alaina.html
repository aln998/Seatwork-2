RAGDOLLS !
  1. LARGE CATS BECAUSE OF THEIR FUR !! <3
  2. Their fur coats are very soft and silky (WOAH like a blanket)
  3. Gentle and calm, especially with children
  4. LOVE LOVE LOVE TO BE CARRIED
  5. Follow you around and PURRFECT for families !!!

British Shorthair !!
  1. Their furs are short and dense
  2. THEIR FACES ARE SO ROUND AND SMALL THEY CAN FIT IN A POINTER-THUMB CIRCLE CUTIESSS
  3. Peaceful, quiet, but also active!
  4. They grow late! They achieve their final size when they turn anytime between 3-5 years of life!
  5. Unlike ragdolls, they are not that needy but become more affectionate when they get to know you more!

Toy Poodle
  1. Quiet, and love roaming about!
  2. Affectionate, LOVE ATTENTION, but do not demand it!
  3. FUR MESS EVERYWHERE! All that cute fur will come shed a TON!
  4. Sociable, friendly, very loving! But for new people, they tend to be unfriendly
  5. Independent, can be left alone by themselves! Though, not for a very long time...
